Amplifying Community Voice Through Strategic Communications

Since 2017, Gallup and the Greater Washington Community Foundation have partnered to understand how residents of the DC, Maryland, and Virginia region experience their communities — their challenges, their hopes, and their priorities for the future. The VoicesDMV Community Insights Report has become a vital resource for philanthropy, policymakers, and community leaders working toward a more equitable and thriving region.

This communications proposal builds on that foundation. The goal is to ensure the next wave of VoicesDMV findings doesn't just sit on a shelf — it drives conversation, informs strategy, and sparks action. Gallup's Strategic Communications team will work alongside GWCF to craft the narrative, build the tools, and execute the activations that maximize the return on your data investment.

The Opportunity

GWCF has made a significant investment in rigorous, community-centered research. The communications strategy outlined here is designed to ensure that investment pays dividends — through media coverage, stakeholder engagement, community ownership of the findings, and lasting digital resources that extend the life and reach of the data well beyond the launch event.

Project Overview — 2026 VoicesDMV

Gallup is partnering with the Greater Washington Community Foundation to design and execute the 2026 VoicesDMV Community Insights initiative — a scientifically rigorous, address-based survey of residents across the DC, Maryland, and Virginia region. The project includes survey development, data collection, advanced analytics, community engagement through an advisory group and in-depth interviews, and a suite of dissemination activities to ensure the findings drive action. The communications services outlined in this proposal are designed to complement and amplify that work.

What This Proposal Adds

The services below represent the communications and activation layer that wraps around the research and community engagement already underway. Each component is independently selectable — build the scope that fits your budget and priorities.
